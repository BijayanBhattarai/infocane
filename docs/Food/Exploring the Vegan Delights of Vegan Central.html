Exploring the Vegan Delights of Vegan Central

Vegan Central is a popular vegan restaurant located in Kathmandu, Nepal, known for its delicious and innovative plant-based cuisine. Among its famous dishes are seitan and choila, which are meat substitutes made from wheat gluten and soy-based meat, respectively.

On my multiple visits to Vegan Central, I've had the pleasure of trying some of their other menu items as well, including the vegan thukpa (noodle soup), choila, and kambucha.

The vegan thukpa is a hearty and satisfying noodle soup that is packed with flavor and nutrients. It is made with fresh vegetables, herbs, and spices, and is often served with a side of noodles. The broth is rich and flavorful, and the dish is perfect for a cold winter day or a rainy evening.

The choila is another standout dish at Vegan Central. It is made with marinated soy-based meat that is seasoned with traditional Nepali spices and served with rice or bread. The dish has a smoky and spicy flavor that is sure to satisfy any craving for meat.

Finally, the kambucha at Vegan Central is a must-try for anyone who loves fermented drinks. Kambucha is a fermented tea that is made with a symbiotic culture of bacteria and yeast. It has a tangy and slightly sweet flavor, and is said to have many health benefits.

Overall, Vegan Central is a great restaurant for anyone who is looking for delicious and nutritious plant-based meals. Whether you are a vegan or simply looking to try something new, there is something for everyone on the menu. I highly recommend trying the seitan choila, as well as the thukpa and kambucha.
